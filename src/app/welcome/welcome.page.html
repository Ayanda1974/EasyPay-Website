About EasyTicketPay

Welcome to EasyTicketPay, the ultimate mobile application crafted to simplify
and automate your trip payments. With our secure and intuitive platform, you can
effortlessly enter your bank card details and the amount you wish to pay, all while
benefiting from the seamless integration of Stripe for payment processing. Our app
boasts a user-friendly interface and a comprehensive ticket management system that
allows you to effortlessly track your purchases. Enjoy the convenience of QR code
scanning for swift in-car payments, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience every
time you travel.
